The Vision

Neo Pisk Motors is leading the energetic transition in the Perené Valley, Peru. Our mission is to harness the immense water resources of the Perené River to produce high-purity Green Hydrogen ($H_2$) via advanced electrolysis. This facility represents the first high-yield hydrogen hub in the Selva Central region.
